Surf vs Nirma: How HUL Tackled their Inexpensive Competition with Atomisation

When Surf faced stiff competition from the low-cost brand Nirma, Hindustan Lever addressed the challenge by atomising the company. “We set up a totally different team of Hindustan Lever managers who worked in a different office.”

Tata Steel's Bold Move into Truck Manufacturing

When Tata Steel identified an opportunity to venture into truck manufacturing, leveraging their steel products, they didn’t simply create a new division under Tata Steel. Instead, they established an entirely separate company.
