"You can't run a large company without proper processes and procedures. Now, how do you do it? That's the question."


R Gopalakrishnan has to his credit an extensive professional journey, spanning over 5 decades. He has shouldered several responsibilities as the Chairman of Unilever Arabia, the Vice Chairman of Hindustan Lever and as the director of TATA Sons. He has also served as an independent director and non-executive chairman of Castrol India and also an independent director of the Press Trust of India.
As a word of growth advice, he emphasises the importance of fostering intrapreneurship in largecompanies by creating separate, independent units or teams with distinct processes. These divisions operate autonomously, allowing them to innovate and take risks without jeopardising the core business, as exemplified by Hindustan Lever and Tata Steel. This strategy enables large organisations to remain agile and competitive while maintaining proper processes and procedures.
